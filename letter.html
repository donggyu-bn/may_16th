Dear friends and family,

Thank you for being here, on our website,
that is not a formality, we meant it.

We did not plan this.
That is the thing about life, you do not plan the parts that matter.
We showed up to church.
We showed up to disability ministries.
We showed up, and then one day
the showing up became about each other,
and after that... as you could imagine...

We are the kind of people who will debate about the flavors in a restaurant.
Who will sit in a café for an entire afternoon,
studying, drinking some form of matcha drinks,
say almost nothing then call it a perfect day.
We have traveled to places we cannot quite remember the names of
and eaten things we will never stop talking about.
This is what love looks like when it is ours.
Ordinarily relentless and full of good food.

Now there will be a wedding.
It will be in Temecula, at a vineyard, in May,
which is the right kind of month for this sort of thing.
But the wedding is not really the point.
You are the point.
You are the people who loved us before we loved each other.
You prayed for us. You stayed.
That is not a small thing.
We know what it costs to stay.

So come.
Come to the vineyard.
Come hungry, because we will be.
Come knowing that your presence is not a detail of this day
but the whole meaning of it.

With love,

Donggyu & Odelia
